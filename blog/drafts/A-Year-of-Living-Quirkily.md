# A Year of Living Quirkily

Overarching theme/title for the blog series as a whole.

## The premise

I'm on sabbatical. *Vis-MLM* is going into production. The question is:
how should I spend my time?

A new R package? Something just for fun — but exciting to think about and
write about?

## What this series is

An umbrella covering Beach Science, Les Chevaliers, and whatever else
shows up — different topics, same underlying spirit.

- **Work-in-progress, not cast in stone.** Posts can evolve over time —
  revised, extended, corrected — rather than being treated as finished
  the moment they're published.
- **A new voice.** Quirky, maybe corny, perhaps a bit cringe-worthy at
  times — but FUN. Different from my usual academic register.
- **An experiment.** I'm willing to encourage this and see where it
  leads, rather than plan it out in advance.

## The other excitement

There's a second kind of excitement I want room for here too: the feeling
of a great statistical graphics idea — mosaic plots, fourfold plots,
corrplots, effect ordering for data display — or writing a lovely R
function or package to produce them, beautifully, with elegant code.

That's a different register from the quirky/corny voice above, but the
same underlying spirit: following what's genuinely exciting rather than
what's expected. Both belong in this sabbatical year.
