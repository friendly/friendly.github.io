# Confessions of a Beach Scientist


Oh, the joys of sitting on Leucate Plage, a lovely Mediteranean beach between Perpigan and Narbonne, backed by
a parasailing cliff and with a view of the Pyranees.

Some fundamental scientific questions can be triggered by the process of "drifting",
lying on your beach towel, eyes closed, opening your mind to interesting or fun scientific questions inspired by the beach ...

One day, I had the idea that I could channel some of this into a new career chunk, by thinking of interesting scientific
questions related to being on a beach. Hey! If nobody has done this before, I could found a new area of research,
**Beach Science**.

Here are a few things that appeared in my consciousness while drifting.

## Clouds and warmth

Q: Lying there, a feel quite a change in temperature and comfort as the clouds drift in and out above me. ..
The beach  scientist might be led to ask:

> What is the relationship between density of cloud cover and perceived warmth? 

* Methods section: How to measure this and record it
  - Let review: how has the sensation of warmth been studies and measured in psychophysics?
  - Now large a sample size of observers to rate "perceived warmth"
  
* Statistical: 
  - Is the relationship simply a linear one?
  - Do we need a multilevel model to take account of individual differences?
